Stonewall

Previously established in Greenwich Village as a tearoom in the 1840's, Stonewall (inn) was later purchased and rebranded as an establishment for gay patrons. Although the clientele were often faced with rampant homophobic police raids. However, on June 28th,1969 a scuffle between patrons and law enforcement kickstarted what came to be known as the gay rights movement with Marsha P.Johnson and Sylvia Rivera being heralded as it's pioneers.
